The Mellow Bathtub


Languages: Python, Flask, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap

Description: This project's purpose is to create an online store. It will display products users (registered or not) can buy. Sellers (registered users as a seller) can add/edit/delete products in the online store. Only sellers of the added product can edit or delete their product from the store. Users can preview items in their shopping cart and their estimated total before deciding to purchase their items. Here they can adjust the quantity of each item or delete items from their cart. Then they will be redirected to a payment page and then redirected to home page once the payment has been submitted.
